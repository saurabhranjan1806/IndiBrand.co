BASMATI RICE

Basmati rice is one of the most well-known and internationally recognized geographic indications that comes from Indian sub-continent. Basmati rice has certain unique characteristics, which owe their origin to the genotype and special agro-climatic conditions prevailing in the Indo- Gangetic Plains. Indi Brand ensures to deliver and share this great treasure of India with its buyers and bridge the gap between communities worldwide.
